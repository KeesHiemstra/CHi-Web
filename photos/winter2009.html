2009-12-20

Even more snow in the garden

That night a new layer of snow had softy been added to the carpet. On some places I have measured 25 cm.

But now the sky wasn't as blue as it has been on Saturday and soon it began snowing again.

Last week I learned a lovely story about one snowflake. When I took the following picture, I thought about it again.


A snowflake...

"Tell me what a snowflake weighs", asked the stoat to the pigeon.
"Nothing more than nothing," was the response of the pigeon.
"In that case I must tell you an amazing story," said the stoat.

"I once sat on a branch of a fir tree, when it gently and quietly began to snow. Because I had nothing better to do, I began to count the snowflakes that fell down on the needles and branch where I sat. I was right at number 3.741.952, when the 3.741.953e snowflake on the branch fell ... Nothing more than nothing, you say, but the branch broke off."
The stoat walked along.

After thinking about the story the dove, which is since Noah's time an authority on peace issues, said to herself: "Perhaps there is only one voice of a man missing for peace in the world to come.

Source unknown, translated from Dutch

At the end of the day I had to travel again. The weather was fine; the road was a little slippery. Only after half an hour the snow started to fall again and sight was then suddenly limited to about 500 m.
